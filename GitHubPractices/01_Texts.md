Self-reminders for text formatting in *.md files

---
# HEADERS

'---' created the thick line above "HEADERS".
# First Header
'# [text]' makes the largest header with line.
## Second Header
'## [text]' makes the second header with line.
### Third Header
'### [text]' makes the third header without line.
#### Fourth Header
'#### [text]' makes the fourth header without line, etc.

---
# Normal Text Format
### Italics
- '_underscore_'
- '*asterisk*'
### Bold Text
- '**double asterisks**'
